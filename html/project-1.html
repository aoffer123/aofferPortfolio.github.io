Fooducate

This application was created to be a simple, innovative and user-friendly solution for efficient nutrition tracking

Project Overview

Project Duration:

January - March 2022

The Problem:

In todayâ€™s fast-paced world, many individuals struggle to maintain a healthy diet due to a lack of time, knowledge, and resources. Traditional methods of meal planning and diet tracking are often cumbersome and not user-friendly, leading to poor adherence to nutritional goals. Furthermore, personalized nutritional guidance is typically inaccessible or expensive for the average person.

Our Solution:

To address these issues, we designed the Fooducate web application, which aims to be a user-friendly, simple, and effective tool for meal planning and diet tracking. The integration of an AI chatbot nutrition coach provides personalized guidance, making healthy eating more accessible and achievable for everyone.

Planning

Main goal identification & Implementation

Our primary objective was to develop an application that promotes healthy eating habits through a streamlined, intuitive user experience. The app addresses and effectively aimed to solve the key issues of existing meal planning and diet tracking tools: complexity and time consumption, lack of personalization, limited accessibility, and poor user engagement.

Our functionality goals included comprehensive meal planning, accurate diet tracking, and an AI-powered chatbot for personalized nutritional guidance. We prioritized tasks in a detailed product backlog and utilized scrum methodology to manage development sprints efficiently. We identified the technology stack to build a robust and user-friendly application included React JS, Firebase, GPT API, Vision AI google, VS code, and github.

This structured planning approach ensured a clear vision and roadmap, guiding us towards the successful development of the Fooducate web application.

Design Process

Research and Wireframes

The design process began with extensive research of existing applications in the market, analyzing competitors to identify best practices and user pain points. This informed our design decisions and allowed us to gather valuable insights. Following this, I created paper wireframes to quickly visualize the layout and functionality of our application, enabling rapid iteration and refinement of our ideas.

After finalizing the paper wireframes, I developed high-fidelity prototypes using Figma, incorporating detailed visual design elements, interactions, and animations to closely mimic the final product. My focus was on creating a visually appealing and user-friendly design. Interactive components were included to simulate user interactions and workflows, enabling thorough testing and validation of the user experience before development. These prototypes were presented to stakeholders for feedback and approval, allowing us to identify potential issues early and make necessary adjustments.

Design Process

High-fidelity prototypes

After finalizing the paper wireframes, I took on the responsibility of developing high-fidelity prototypes using advanced design tools. These prototypes included detailed visual design elements, interactions, and animations to closely mimic the final product. My focus was on ensuring the design was visually appealing and user-friendly. The prototypes were created using Figma, which allowed for precise control over the design elements and seamless collaboration with the development team.

In addition to visual elements, I incorporated interactive components to simulate user interactions and workflows. This allowed for thorough testing and validation of the user experience before moving into the development phase. The high-fidelity prototypes were presented to stakeholders for feedback and approval, ensuring that all design aspects met user needs and expectations. This process was crucial for identifying any potential issues early and making necessary adjustments to create a seamless and intuitive final product.

Implementation

As the lead front-end developer, I was responsible for translating the high-fidelity wireframes into functional code for each page of the application. Utilizing React JS and the MUI component library, I implemented a responsive and interactive user interface. This involved creating reusable components, managing state with React's hooks, and ensuring a seamless user experience across different devices and screen sizes. My focus was on maintaining clean, modular code and adhering to best practices in front-end development.
